Passé Composé

Master the Passé Composé tense with these interactive lessons.

Introduction

The Passé Composé is used to describe actions that have been completed in the past. It's a compound tense formed using the auxiliary verbs avoir or être and the past participle of the verb.

Examples:

  • J'ai mangé (I ate)
  • Tu as parlé (You spoke)
  • Il a fini (He finished)
